percentages, not reasons.

Customers pick the first option and skip the text field. You learn nothing you can act on.

Old way — static surveys
They pick the first answer

No follow-up is possible

A static form can't ask "is it price or did the team never adopt it?" The depth simply isn't there.

30% said pricing. So what? Knowing the breakdown doesn't tell you what to change.

The only offer you have is a discount

Without knowing the real reason, you can't match a solution. So you cut price and train the wrong behavior.

How it works

One script tag.
Running in minutes.

No backend changes. No SDK. Paste it into your cancellation page and Last Word handles the rest.

01 — Deploy

Add the widget

One script tag with your API key. The widget runs in an isolated iframe — never conflicts with your CSS, never blocks a cancellation. API failures degrade silently.

02 — Configure

Tell it about your product

Paste your URL to auto-extract logo and brand colors. Set product name, plans, and which competitors to track. The AI uses all of it to personalize every interview.

03 — Automate

AI runs every interview

3–5 exchanges per customer. Casual, human, never defensive. The AI adapts based on plan, account age, MRR, and seats — high-value accounts get a different response.

04 — Learn

Conversations become intelligence

Category, deep reasons, key quote, competitor mentions, feature gaps, sentiment — structured and stored automatically. Slack alerts for high-value cancellations.
