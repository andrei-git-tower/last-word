Problem

Cancellation surveys
are broken by design.

When customers cancel, they click the first option and skip the text field. You get percentages, not reasons.

✗  Old way: static surveys
Customers pick the nearest answer "Too expensive" gets 40% of clicks — not because it's true, but because it's first on the list.
No follow-up possible A checkbox can't ask "is it price, or did the team never adopt it?" You never dig past the surface.
Useless aggregate data Knowing 30% said "pricing" tells you nothing about what to fix. Retention requires specifics, not tallies.
Blunt retention attempts Without knowing the real reason, the only offer you have is a discount — which trains the wrong behavior.
vs
✓  Last Word: AI conversations
A real conversation follows up The AI hears "too expensive," then asks "is that a budget thing, or did the team not end up using it?" and gets to the truth.
Reflective listening that goes deeper 3–5 exchanges in a casual, human tone. Curious, never defensive. Gets to the actual cancellation driver.
Structured insight on every interview Category, deep reasons, sentiment, key quote, feature gaps, competitor mentions — extracted automatically.
The right retention path, not a discount Match the real problem to the right offer: pause, downgrade, fix & follow up, concierge, or graceful offboard.

Integration

One script tag.
Running in minutes.

No SDK to install. No backend changes. Drop it in and Last Word handles the rest.

01
deploy

Add the widget to your cancellation page

One script tag with your API key. Runs in an isolated iframe so it never conflicts with your CSS or blocks a cancellation. If the API fails, the iframe fails silently — your flow is never interrupted.

02
configure

Tell Last Word about your product

Paste your URL for auto-branding (logo + colors extracted automatically). Set your product name, plans, competitors to watch, and retention path rules. The AI uses all of it to personalize every interview.

03
automate

The AI runs every interview, automatically

3–5 exchanges per customer. Casual, human, never defensive. The AI adapts based on the user's plan, account age, MRR, and seat count — higher-value accounts get a different response.

04
learn

Every conversation becomes structured intelligence

Category, deep reasons, key quote, competitor mentions, feature gaps, sentiment — extracted automatically and stored. Slack alerts for high-value cancellations. Everything flows into your dashboard.

Retention paths

Five solutions.
Zero discounts.

PATH-02

Downgrade Plan

PATH-03

Fix & Follow Up
