Exit interview · Alex from Acme Co
🤖
Hey Alex — before you go, I'd love to understand what's not working for you. What's the main reason you're cancelling today?
A
Honestly, it's too expensive for what we get.
🤖
That makes sense. When you say it's not worth the price — is it that the features you use most aren't there, or more that the whole team never really adopted it?
A
Honestly the second one. We set it up but only I ever used it. The rest of the team kept using Linear for everything.
🤖
Got it — so the real issue is adoption, not price. Would it help if someone from our team did a 20-minute onboarding session with your whole team before you leave?
